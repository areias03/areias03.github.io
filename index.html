    I'm a PhD Student in the Centre for Microbiome Research at Queensland University of Technology in Brisbane, Australia. I have an enormous passion for biology and informatics, which allows me to create a perfect balance and explore the maximum of my capabilities, while doing two things I have an tremendous interest in. Trying to keep on learning and improving is one of my personal mottoes, in the endless journey of creating a wiser and better version of myself.
